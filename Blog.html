Meet My Family

I was raised in Augusta by my parents, Debbie and Brian Viens. My mother is a kindergarden teacher and my father works in the greenhouse department of Lowe's. They embody what I would like to see for my life. They both love what they do, and they love coming home to talk about their day.


Something that my father onece told me,"If you love what you do Nate, you will never work a day in your life."


This being said, there is something more important to what he said. My father has a trio of disorders, Retinitis Pigmentosa, Ushers Syndrome, and Cerebellar Ataxia. These syndrones have made him legally blind, allowing him to only see out of the equivalent of a pin hole, hard of hearing and also with muscle and coordination issues. However, this has not stopped him.

My brother, Dand and his wife Larissa live in Augusta as well with their two children, Riley and Calvin. I have looked up to them for as long as I can remember. Dan, being six years older than myself, has had opportunities that I always wished I had, but I have been too young. Figuring out that we both look up to each other was hard for us, but was very enlightening. I don't get to see them as often as I should, as being in school and working full time to pay for it, my girlfriend and I try to get down to Augusta as often as we can.

My best friends have always been there for me. Colin, Anna and Beal still remain my best friends from High School. Being so close, I have been able to see Colin and Anna a lot more often. I miss Matt Beal who was always the clown and goofball in the group.
