Hruthmund Arûna

Sent from the High King of the Northern Kingdom, Hruthmund, a dwarf cleric from the Halls of Broderik, has set out from the Six Lands to aid the Righteous Few to banish the Knotring Blight, a scourge on the land that seems to have no end. Hruthmund was raised with the teachings of Broderik Highmahtern, the God of all Dwarven. Seeking to prove himself to his God, Hruthmund now protects all in his party as they attempt to remove this curse from their lands.
